About ARISE

AI-based Reading and Inquiry Support Engine (ARISE) is an automated question answering system for textbooks, research papers, and other educational materials. The system will take any kind of document as an input and generate answers to a set of questions related to the content. ARISE is able to perform three tasks based on the requirement of user ,i.e., long form, short-form and tabular form question-answering.
The Key Features:
1. Efficiency: Can quickly and accurately generate answers. Users can save time and effort in searching for information manually.
2. Accessibility: Individuals with varying levels of expertise or different learning styles can easily access and comprehend complex information.
3. Personalized Learning: Users can ask specific questions related to their areas of interest or areas they find challenging, facilitating a deeper understanding of the subject matter.
4. Enhanced Learning Experience: interface promoting an interactive learning experience.

Our Services

Long Form QnA

Provide accurate and detailed answers for the provided question. The answer will be closely related to the document provided.

Short Form QnA

Provide precise and most suitable one line answers for the provided question. Helps in getting brief information on the required topic.

Tabular Form QnA

Helps in extracting the required information from the data provided in tabular form i.e. Excel Sheet, csv files etc.
